Classic Cheese Omelette

A simple yet delicious omelette filled with rich, melted cheddar cheese. Perfect for a quick breakfast or a lazy weekend brunch.

Preparation time

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2-3cup shredded cheddar
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: diced veggies
  • Optional: fresh herbs
  • Optional: cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Whisk eggs with salt and pepper until combined. Add a splash of milk for a fluffier result.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt the butter until bubbling.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the egg mixture. Tilt the pan so the eggs coat the surface evenly.
  4. Add fillings: When edges set but the centre is still slightly runny, sprinkle cheddar over one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half, cook one more minute, then slide onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve immediately with extra salt and pepper if needed.
